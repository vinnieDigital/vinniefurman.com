About

Vinnie Furman is a freelance digital designer with over 10 years of professional design experience, including 5 years producing code & design in a fast-paced production environment. He has worked with agencies and corporations as well as small-businesses, non-profits and individuals to provide concise solutions to their creative design and web needs. When not behind his trusty Macbook, Vinnie enjoys drawing, cycling, hiking, taking in local music shows and creating lo-fi hip-hop beats.
